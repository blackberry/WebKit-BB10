Javascript media

This is a sequence of tests to verify the behaviour of the media elements as seen by javascript. In the following test description, "immediately" means "after one click":

  • Open the http://www.scottandrew.com/pub/html5audioplayer/ test file.
  • Click on the play button, and verify that it starts to play immediately.
  • Click on the fast forward or fast buttons, and verify that the next or previous track begins to play immediately.
  • Click on the pause and play buttons, and verify that the media pauses and restarts.
  • Let one track play to the end, and verify that the next track starts authomatically.
