Fullscreen Video

One test case:

  1. Start the video
  2. While the video is playing, click on the media player control's to make it full screen
  3. After the video has stopped playing, while the media player is full-screened, try various things:
    • Stop and restart the video
    • Change the volume
    • Rotate the device (the fullscreen mode doesn't rotate with the device; the non-fullscreen, in-page mode does rotate).
