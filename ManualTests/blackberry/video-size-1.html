Sized Video #1

This is a video element which has no explicit height and width attributes. The intrinsic size of the media file is smaller (320x240).

When you start the video, verify that the size of the media player increases from its default 300x150 to the video's intrinsic size of 320x240.

Also verify that the controls on the control bar look correctly placed before and after the video is played, and that the scrolling thumb-bar reaches its right-hand end just as the 'time remaining' reaches "00:00", and the video comes to an end.
