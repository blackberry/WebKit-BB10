Sized Video #4

This is a video element with explicit width=400 and height=400 attributes. The intrinsic size of the media file is smaller (320x240).

The aspect ratio of the video element and of the video are different: the window is tall and narrow compared to the video.

When you start the video, verify that the width of the video matches the width of the containing rectangle. Also verify that the height of the video is smaller than the height of the containing rectangle. Also verify that the location of the video is vertically centered in the containing rectangle and that the controls are placed at the bottom of the containing rectangle.
