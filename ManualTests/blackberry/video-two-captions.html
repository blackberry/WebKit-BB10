Two Captions

Use this to test switching between two videos, one which includes a caption:

  • Press the play to start one video
  • Without stopping the first video, start the second video (the first video should stop automatically)
  • Without stopping the second video, start the first video (the second video should stop automatically)
  • Try the above repeatedly: verify that the caption window is displayed whenever the first video is playing, and verify that there is no browser crash.
